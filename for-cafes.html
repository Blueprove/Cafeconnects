The challenge

Cafés Face Real Challenges in 2026

The specialty coffee market is booming — but running a profitable café is harder than ever. CafeConnects was built specifically to help you solve these problems.

📉

Low Repeat Visitor Rate

Most cafés see less than 20% repeat visitors. Customers come once, have a good experience, and don't return for weeks — or ever. There's no mechanism to bring them back.

📅

Empty Weekdays

Thursday mornings, Tuesday afternoons — dead. You have the space, the baristas, and the coffee ready, but no foot traffic. Weekends barely make up for it.

👥

No Community Around Your Café

Your regulars don't know each other. Every customer sits in isolation. You want to host events but have no community to invite — no engaged audience to market to.

📊

Zero Data on Your Customers

You have no idea who your customers are, what they're interested in, or when they're most likely to return. Your business decisions are based entirely on gut feeling.

Our Solution

CafeConnects Solves All of This.

By turning your café into a connection hub, we create an intrinsic reason for people to return — to see if that interesting person they poked is back. It's a powerful, organic pull.

🔄

Drive Repeat Visits

Cafés partnered with us see 35-45% repeat visitor rates — users come back hoping to reconnect with someone they poked.

📊

Real Analytics Dashboard

See daily active users, match counts, peak hours, interest demographics, and estimated revenue attribution — live.

🎉

Fill Weekdays with Events

Co-branded "Poke Nights" curated for your café's vibe — startup nights, creative meetups, book clubs. We promote. You host.

📣

Free Co-Marketing

We feature partner cafés in our app, newsletter, Instagram, and blog. 5,000+ engaged users see your café every week.
