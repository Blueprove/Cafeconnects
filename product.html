How CafeConnects Works

The Product Built for Real Connections.

Five thoughtful steps from "sitting near someone interesting" to "actually having a genuine conversation." Every design choice prioritises safety, privacy, and authenticity.

Step 1

Discover People Around You in Cafés

Open CafeConnects at any partner café. Using precise proximity detection (within 10 metres), the app shows you anonymised profiles of people who are physically nearby — matching your declared interests.

No photos. No full names. Just a bio, age, and the interests you have in common. We designed it this way deliberately: connect based on who someone is, not what they look like.

  • See only people within 10 metres of you
  • Profiles ranked by shared interest overlap
  • Photos hidden until mutual interest confirmed
  • Works at 120+ partner cafés across Bangalore

Step 2

Send a Poke

Interested in someone? Tap "Poke" on their profile. A Poke is a gentle, low-pressure signal — think of it as a friendly acknowledgement, not a romantic advance.

The other person receives a discreet notification: "Someone at Blue Tokai is interested in you." They can choose to poke back, ignore it, or block. There's absolutely no pressure.

  • Anonymous notification — they know someone poked, not who
  • 24-hour cooldown prevents spamming
  • Pokes expire in 7 days if not reciprocated
  • One-tap block removes someone permanently

Step 3

Mutual Interest Unlocks Conversation

When both of you have poked each other, the first lock breaks: photos become visible and an ephemeral chat opens. This chat exists only while you're both within 10 metres.

The proximity requirement is intentional — it keeps conversations grounded in the real world. Walk away, and the chat closes. Walk back in, and the warmth picks up right where it left off.

  • Photos unlock for both parties simultaneously
  • In-app chat only within 10 metres
  • Messages are ephemeral — deleted when session ends
  • The proximity rule encourages face-to-face conversation

Step 4

Build Genuine Connections Over Time

Connections deepen naturally across multiple café visits. After a 2nd mutual poke, the bond strengthens. After a 3rd — the connection fully unlocks: persistent chat, contact info sharing, and a permanent record of your friendship story.

This gradual progression mirrors how real friendships form — not through an algorithm, but through repeated, organic encounters at a place you both love.

  • Poke 1: Ephemeral in-café chat
  • Poke 2: Connection deepens, streak builds
  • Poke 3: Full unlock — persistent chat + contact sharing
  • Your friendship story is tied to the café you met at

Step 5

Safety and Privacy at Every Step

Safety isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation every feature is built on. From day one, we've made decisions that prioritise your wellbeing over engagement metrics.

  • ID verification before your first poke
  • Trust score system visible on every profile
  • Location data deleted the moment you close the app
  • Instant block — permanent, cross-account
  • Report abuse — reviewed within 24 hours
  • Auto-suspend after 3 verified reports in 7 days

Platform Features

Every Feature Has a Reason

Nothing here exists for engagement metrics. Every feature was built to make real-world connections safer, more natural, and more meaningful.

👤

Anonymous Profiles

Initial profiles show only age, pronouns, bio, and shared interests. Photos are a reward for mutual curiosity — not a hook for first impressions.

📍

Proximity Chat

In-café chat requires both users to stay within 10 metres. This is our favourite feature — it makes digital interaction an extension of the physical space.

🌱

The 3-Poke Journey

Three mutual pokes across café visits. Each poke deepens trust gradually — mirroring how real friendships actually develop over time and repeated encounters.

🔐

Location Privacy Engine

Real-time location held in memory, never written to disk. When you close the app, your position is gone. No trails. No history. No surveillance.

⭐

Trust Score System

A community-driven trust score (0-100) reflects your history of positive interactions. High-trust users see more profiles. Low-trust users see fewer. It self-regulates.

📣

Interest Matching

Profiles ranked by Jaccard similarity — the overlap between your declared interests and theirs. The more you have in common, the higher they appear in your feed.
