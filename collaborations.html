Influencers & Creators

You create content about real life, travel, food, coffee, or community. We help you take your audience somewhere real — literally.

  • Co-branded Poke Night experiences
  • Early access to platform features
  • Revenue sharing on referred café sign-ups
  • Featured in our weekly newsletter

Coffee Brands

Your beans deserve to be savoured during real, meaningful conversations — not alone in front of a phone screen. Let's make that happen.

  • In-app brand placement at partner cafés
  • Co-hosted brand events
  • Featured in café discovery section
  • Social media co-marketing

Communities & Clubs

Book clubs, startup networks, design collectives, sports groups — we help you find new members in real physical spaces.

  • Custom interest filter for your community
  • Dedicated "Poke Night" events for your group
  • Partnership listing in our app
  • QR code campaigns to grow membership
🎪

Events & Experiences

Conferences, pop-ups, art shows, open mics — we can make your events social discovery opportunities for people who share passions.

  • Geo-fenced matching at your event
  • Special event mode in-app
  • Co-promotion to our Bangalore user base
  • Post-event connection facilitation
🎓

Universities & Colleges

Fresh out of college and new to Bangalore? We help students build real social networks, not just follower counts.

  • Campus activation programmes
  • Student ambassador network
  • Events partnership with student bodies
  • Co-branded orientation experiences

Media & Press

Writing about loneliness, social tech, Bangalore's startup scene, or café culture? We'd love to tell you our story.
