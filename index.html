Questions

Frequently Asked

Is CafeConnects a dating app?
+
Absolutely not. CafeConnects is a social discovery platform designed for building genuine friendships, professional connections, and community. While you may organically develop any kind of relationship, our design philosophy is rooted in casual, interest-based connections — not romantic pursuit.
Is my location being tracked or stored?
+
Never stored long-term. Your location is only active in real-time when the app is open, held temporarily in a cache for matching, and deleted the moment you close the app. We never build a historical record of where you've been. Your café check-in is anonymised.
Why can't I see photos immediately?
+
Photos unlock only after a mutual poke — when both parties have expressed interest. This is a deliberate design choice to ensure connections are based on shared interests and genuine curiosity, not appearance. It creates a much healthier dynamic and reduces superficial judgement.
What is a "Poke" exactly?
+
A Poke is a low-pressure signal of interest — like a gentle tap on the shoulder. It says "hey, I noticed you're here and our interests seem to align." There's no obligation for the other person to respond. It's designed to be friendly and non-invasive.
How does the 3-poke system work?
+
Each visit builds the connection naturally: Poke 1 (mutual interest confirmed, ephemeral chat opens), Poke 2 (deeper engagement, connection strengthens), Poke 3 (full unlock — persistent chat, contact sharing enabled). This gradual progression mirrors how real friendships form organically over time.
Which cafés in Bangalore are supported?
+
We currently have 120+ partner cafés across Indiranagar, Koramangala, HSR Layout, Whitefield, and more. See our full Bangalore café map for the complete list with ratings, timings, and categories. New cafés join every week.
Is the app free to use?
+
Yes — CafeConnects is free to download and use. We offer a Premium tier (₹99/month) that unlocks additional features like extended poke range, priority visibility, and advanced interest filters. All core connection features are available for free.
