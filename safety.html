How We Protect You

Safety Built Into Every Layer

🪪

ID Verification

Every user must verify their identity before sending their first poke. This is mandatory — not optional. We use Onfido's AI-powered verification to confirm government IDs without storing sensitive documents.

🖼️

Photos Hidden Until Mutual

Profile photos are never visible until both parties have expressed mutual interest. This prevents appearance-based harassment and ensures connections are built on who someone is — not how they look.

📍

Location Privacy Engine

Your precise location is held only in real-time memory, never written to our database. When you close the app, your location is gone. We never build a history of where you've been or when.

🚫

Instant Block — Permanent

One tap blocks someone forever. Blocked users can never poke you, see your profile, or contact you — even if they create a new account. Our system links blocks to identity, not just accounts.

⭐

Trust Score System

Every user has a Trust Score (0-100) based on their interaction history. Users who receive blocks or reports see their score drop and their visibility reduced. The community self-regulates organically.

⚡

Auto-Moderation

A user who receives 3 verified reports within 7 days is automatically suspended pending review. Our moderation team reviews every report within 24 hours and takes action within 48.

Stay Safe

Tips for Safe, Positive Interactions

☕ Meeting in Person

Always meet for the first time in a public café — which, by design, is exactly where CafeConnects connections happen.
You don't have to identify yourself. You can make eye contact and smile, or simply enjoy knowing someone interesting is nearby.
Never feel pressured to reveal your identity until you're genuinely comfortable doing so.
Trust your gut. If something feels off about an interaction, block immediately. No explanation needed.
Let a friend or family member know you're at the café if you plan to introduce yourself to someone.

🔒 Privacy & Boundaries

Never share personal contact info (phone, social media) until the chat is fully unlocked after 3 mutual pokes — and only if you want to.
You don't owe anyone a poke back. Ignoring a poke is completely normal and there are no notifications that tell someone you've seen their poke.
Your profile only shows what you choose to share. Age, pronouns, bio, and interests are all under your control.
Respect is not optional. Inappropriate chat messages should be reported immediately — we act fast.
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings. No cooling-off period.
Our Standards

Community Standards

CafeConnects is a community built on respect, curiosity, and kindness. These standards are non-negotiable.

🤝

Respect Everyone

Every person on this platform deserves to be treated with dignity. Harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behaviour of any kind results in immediate suspension.

🚫

No Unsolicited Content

Do not send inappropriate images, offensive messages, or unsolicited advances. Chat is for friendly, interest-based conversation — not anything else.

✅

Be Authentically You

No fake profiles, no impersonation, no catfishing. Your profile should represent who you genuinely are. Misrepresentation is grounds for permanent ban.

🌍

Embrace Diversity

Our community includes people of all backgrounds, identities, and beliefs. Discrimination based on gender, religion, caste, sexuality, or any other identity is absolutely prohibited.

🔔

Report, Don't Retaliate

If someone violates these standards, report them immediately in-app. Do not engage, argue, or retaliate. Our team will handle it.

☕

Respect Café Space

Be a good guest in the cafés where you connect. Treat staff with kindness, be mindful of space, and remember you're representing our community.

Reporting

How to Report Abuse

1

Tap the profile or chat

Open the profile or conversation of the person you want to report. You can do this from anywhere in the app.

2

Tap the ⋯ menu → "Report"

Select the type of issue: harassment, inappropriate content, spam, fake profile, or unsafe behaviour.

3

Add details (optional but helpful)

Any additional context helps us act faster and more accurately. Screenshots can be attached.

✓

We handle it from here

Your report is reviewed within 24 hours. Serious cases are escalated immediately. You'll receive a notification when action is taken.

🚨 In an Emergency, Call Immediately

CafeConnects is not an emergency service. If you're ever in danger, call emergency services directly. Do not wait to file an in-app report.
